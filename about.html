Our story

From The Block To The Boards

Ground Up Entertainment started the way most great things in Chicago do — with hunger, a shared studio, and a refusal to wait for permission. We watched too many talented artists get overlooked because they didn't have the rooms, the team, or the guidance. So we built it.

Today GUE is a full-service label and studio: a place where an artist can walk in with a voice memo and walk out with a release plan. We invest in people first and records second, because the catalog takes care of itself when the artist is built right.

🎯

Mission

To give independent artists the tools, team, and treatment of a major label — without losing ownership of their art or their voice.

👁️

Vision

A Chicago-rooted label whose roster defines the next sound of the city and proves you can build a real career from the ground up.

⚖️

Values

Honesty over hype. Development over shortcuts. Loyalty to the artists who trust us with their sound and their story.
